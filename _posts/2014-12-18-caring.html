--- title: "Does CSS Performance Matter?" layout: post ---

Yes it does.

It matters because performance matters in general. You might care about performance for different reasons, but I can’t think of a single reason you wouldn’t care at all.

I care about performance because I think a users time is the most valuable thing and shouldn’t be wasted for any reason. You might care about performance because you want to make more money.

I just think you should care.
